About Me

I am Prasanna Kumar, currently pursuing my MCA in my first year at SJB Institute of Technology in Bangalore, Karnataka, India. I have over two years of experience at Cognizant, from November 2021 to September 2023, where I worked on high-impact production projects, handling both web and desktop applications. My responsibilities included resolving daily incidents, conducting application health checks, attending and managing change requests, and resolving issues locally.
